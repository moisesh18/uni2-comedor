Help Oaxaca

On September 7, 2017 almost at midnight the ground began to shake. Asunción Ixtaltepec, Oaxaca was one of the most affected areas. Our school “Benito Juárez” presented structural damage in nearly 100% of the building. Civil Protection of the State of Oaxaca has deemed the building uninhabitable and has detained regular activities.

With a heavy heart, the administration began the demolition of the property, with faith that God will provide.

We need 7 million pesos for the reconstruction of the new school. The Student Federation and Youth Ministry of the University of Montemorelos has begun to do its part, our goal is $ 201,600.00 but by God’s grace and your help, we know we can reach even more.
